Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2024

Welcome to RentMatch. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Full name, age, phone number, profile photo, bio, occupation
  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens (if using Google/Apple sign-in), session data

1.2 Rental Preferences

  • Budget range (minimum and maximum)
  • Preferred locations
  • Lifestyle preferences (e.g., pet-friendly, gym access, parking)
  • Property type preferences

1.3 Listing Information (For Property Owners)

  • Property details (address, description, amenities)
  • Property photos
  • Rental price and availability
  • Contact information

1.4 Usage Data

  • App interactions (swipes, likes, matches)
  • Messages and chat history
  • Search queries and filters
  • Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
  • Location data (when you grant permission)

1.5 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Push notification tokens
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, match you with relevant properties or tenants
  • Communication: Send notifications about matches, messages, and important updates
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on your preferences
  • Safety & Security: Verify identities, prevent fraud, and maintain platform safety
  • Analytics: Improve our services, understand user behavior, and fix technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 With Other Users

When you match with another user or express interest in a property, we share your profile information and messages with that user.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Cloud Storage: For storing photos and media
  • Push Notification Services: To send you app notifications
  • Analytics Providers: To understand app usage and improve services

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.4 Business Transfers

If RentMatch is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications or marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

Delete Your Account

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information are permanently deleted
  • Your messages and chat history are removed
  • Your photos and media files are deleted from our servers
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records)

7. Children's Privacy

RentMatch is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

8. Location Data

We collect location data only when you grant permission. Location data is used to:

  • Show properties near you
  • Filter search results by distance
  • Provide location-based recommendations

You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage
  • Improve performance

10. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this privacy policy.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of RentMatch after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@rentmatch.app
  • Support: support@rentmatch.app
