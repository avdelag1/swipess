Delete Your RentMatch Account

We're sorry to see you go

This page provides information about deleting your RentMatch account and all associated data. Please read this carefully before proceeding.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Name, email, phone number, bio, photos
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and OAuth tokens
  • Property Listings: All your property listings and associated photos (for property owners)
  • Messages: All your conversations and chat history
  • Matches: All your matches and swipe history
  • Preferences: Saved searches, filters, and preferences
  • Media Files: All photos and documents you've uploaded
  • Activity Data: Your usage history and interactions
  • Notifications: Your notification preferences and history

üìã Data Retention for Legal Purposes

Some limited data may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction records (required by law)
  • Records of reported violations or safety incidents
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data

This data cannot be linked back to you personally and does not include your profile information.

How to Delete Your Account

üì± Method 1: Delete Through the Mobile App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is through the RentMatch mobile app:

  1. Open the RentMatch App

    Launch the RentMatch app on your device and make sure you're logged in.

  2. Go to Settings

    Navigate to your profile and tap on the Settings icon or menu.

  3. Find Account Settings

    Scroll down to find "Profile Settings" or "Account Settings".

  4. Locate the Danger Zone

    Scroll to the bottom of the settings page to find the "Danger Zone" section.

  5. Delete Account

    Tap "Delete Account Permanently" and confirm your decision in the dialog that appears.

  6. Confirmation

    You'll receive a confirmation message and will be logged out automatically.

üìß Method 2: Contact Support

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact our support team:

  • Email: support@rentmatch.app
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered email address and username

We'll process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email.

Timeline for Data Deletion

  • Immediate: Your account is deactivated and you're logged out
  • Within 24 hours: Your profile becomes invisible to other users
  • Within 7 days: All personal data is permanently deleted from active databases
  • Within 30 days: Data is removed from backup systems

üîÑ Alternatives to Account Deletion

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate Temporarily: Hide your profile without deleting data
  • Update Privacy Settings: Control what information is visible
  • Disable Notifications: Stop receiving app notifications
  • Clear Preferences: Reset your search filters and preferences

After Account Deletion

After your account is deleted:

  • You cannot sign in to the app
  • Other users will not see your profile or messages
  • You cannot recover any data
  • You can create a new account with the same email after deletion is complete
  • Previous matches will lose access to your information
